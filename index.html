We are registered under NDIS Provider in Northern Territory

Northern Support Services is an NDIS provider in NT which was established in 2023.

We are acknowledged as one of the fastest growing NDIS registered providers. A proud Territorian, the business was founded in Katherine and a year later expanded the business in Darwin. Services provided includes supported accommodation, SIL, assistance in daily living, access to community, respite, and capacity building. Our main goal is to provide quality service to people with disability to help them live an ordinary life. Services

Daily Living Support
We offer personalized help with daily activities to ensure your routines run smoothly. We are here to support you in maintaining your independence and quality of life.
Social, Economic, and Community Engagement
Northern Support Services is more than just about activities; it's about connection. We help you connect with your community and engage in social and economic activities.
Enhanced Community Participation
We focus on increasing social and community involvement by providing tailored strategies and support. Our team will guide you through joining social groups or participating in community events, ensuring you feel included and enjoy a more fulfilling life.
