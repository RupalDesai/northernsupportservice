About Us

Dear Friends, Community Members, and Valued Partners,

Welcome to Northern Support Services. My name is Trevor Pinto, and as the CEO of Northern Support Services, I am honoured and excited to share our story with you. Our journey began with a simple yet profound belief: every individual deserves to live a life of dignity, independence, and joy. Here in the Northern Territory, where our vast landscapes mirror our diverse communities, we are committed to providing personalized, compassionate support to those most need it.

At Northern Support Services, we stand out as more than just an NDIS provider. We are your partners in navigating the path to a better tomorrow. Our unique approach, grounded in respect, empathy, and empowerment, sets us apart. We understand that each person's needs are unique, so we tailor our services to fit the goals and aspirations of the individuals and families we serve.

A shared vision drives our dedicated team: to enhance the quality of life for our clients through innovative, responsive, and client-centered care. We pride ourselves on our strong local presence and deep understanding of the Northern Territory's unique challenges and opportunities. This enables us to deliver support that is not only effective but truly meaningful.

As we continue to grow and evolve, our unwavering commitment to you remains steadfast. We are here to listen, to adapt, and to ensure that you have the support you need to achieve your goals and dreams. Our adaptability ensures that you can always rely on us, no matter how your needs may change.

We sincerely appreciate your interest in Northern Support Services. We are eager for the opportunity to work with you and to be a part of your journey toward a more fulfilling and independent life.

Warm regards,

Trevor Pinto

Founder & CEO
